I am a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering at Harvard John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. My research focuses on multimodal AI for biomedical sciences. I am primarily based in the Department of Radiology at Massachusetts General Hospital. I am fortunate to be mentored by and collaborate with many outstanding scholars.

I received my bachelor's and master’s degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University. Before starting my PhD, I worked as a full-stack software engineer. When not working, I hunt for the best freshly roasted coffee beans in the greater Boston area. You might also find me catching a show at Central Square Theater or the Huntington Theatre.
